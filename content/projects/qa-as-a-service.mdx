A detailed write-up of this engagement is in progress. In the meantime, the description above and the highlights captured the headline results.

## What I built

- Scoped retainers with clear deliverables, so prospects know exactly what they're buying before the first call.
- A repeatable onboarding kit (CI templates, reporting harness, playbook docs) that turns the first 2 weeks of every engagement into a tee-up rather than a build-from-scratch.
- A reporting stack — Playwright artifacts piped into Azure DevOps, aggregated into a Power BI workspace — that makes test coverage and flake rate visible to non-engineering stakeholders.
